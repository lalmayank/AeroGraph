# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making AeroGraph a welcoming, respectful, and productive project for everyone, regardless of background, identity, or experience level.

## Expected Behavior

Contributors should:

- Be respectful and constructive in code review, issues, and discussion.
- Focus feedback on ideas and implementation, not on people.
- Give and receive feedback with patience and clarity.
- Respect project maintainers, reviewers, and other contributors' time.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of behavior that is not acceptable include:

- Harassment, intimidation, or abuse.
- Discriminatory, hateful, or exclusionary language.
- Personal attacks or derailing discussion with bad-faith conduct.
- Sharing private information without permission.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to project spaces, including issues, pull requests, reviews, and any project-hosted discussion channels.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject content that violates this code of conduct and may restrict participation when needed to protect the community.

If you experience or witness a violation, please raise it through the project maintainers or the repository's established contact path.
